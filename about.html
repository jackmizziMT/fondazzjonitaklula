About

The Fondazzjoni
ta' Klula

A voluntary organisation based in Santa Lucija, Gozo — dedicated to heritage, culture, and community.

Our Story

How it all
began

Fondazzjoni ta' Klula — VO/0420 — was founded by a group of Santa Lucija residents committed to ensuring that the village's heritage is not lost to time. The name comes from Klula, the affectionate local name for the village.

We are a registered voluntary organisation operating under the Commissioner for Voluntary Organisations. Our work spans cultural events, heritage documentation, oral history, arts programming, and community engagement.

What began as a commitment to one village has grown into something broader: a belief that Gozitan culture, in all its forms, deserves to be documented, celebrated, and shared — with everyone who's curious enough to look.

What We Stand For

Our objectives

Five principles guide everything the Fondazzjoni does — from a single literary evening to a long-term heritage documentation project.

01

Safeguarding the tangible and intangible cultural heritage of Santa Lucija

02

Promoting the village's history, traditions, and natural environment

03

Supporting arts and cultural activities that engage the local community

04

Building a welcoming space for residents, visitors, and the wider public

05

Fostering partnerships with local, national, and international organisations
