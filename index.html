Who We Are

A cultural foundation rooted in Gozo

Fondazzjoni ta' Klula is a voluntary organisation dedicated to safeguarding and celebrating Gozo's cultural heritage. Rooted in Santa Lucija, we bring communities together through events, arts, oral history, and shared traditions — for residents, visitors, and anyone who loves this island.

"Gozo's heritage is alive in its people, its streets, its celebrations — and it belongs to everyone."

What We Do

Heritage, culture, community

Three pillars, one mission — keeping Gozo's culture alive.

🏛

Heritage

Documenting and preserving the stories, traditions, and places that make Gozo's communities unique.

🎭

Culture

From literary evenings to open-air events, we bring cultural experiences to life — rooted in the village, with an outlook across Gozo.

🤝

Community

Rooted in Santa Lucija, welcoming to all. Our events are designed for everyone — locals and visitors alike.
